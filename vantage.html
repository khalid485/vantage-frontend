2. Information We Collect

What you provide:

  • Email address — for authentication and service communications only.
  • Creator username — used solely to perform your requested scan. Not stored beyond active session unless you initiate a dashboard audit.
  • Payment info — handled entirely by Paddle. We never see or store your card details.

Collected automatically:

  • IP address — for fraud prevention, not linked to identity, purged after 30 days.
  • Browser and device type — for security monitoring via standard server logs.
  • Usage data — pages visited, scans initiated. Used to improve the product. Never sold.

What we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect, store, or access any of your actual content (images, videos, files).
  • We do not track you across other websites.
  • We do not use advertising cookies or third-party ad networks.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate your identity.
  • To perform leak scans and send automated DMCA takedown notices on your behalf.
  • To send transactional emails — magic links, scan reports, enforcement updates.
  • To process your subscription through Paddle and manage billing.
  • To detect abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.
  • To improve our detection algorithms and enforcement systems.
  • To comply with legal obligations and lawful requests from authorities.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose not listed above.

  • Performance of a contract — processing necessary to provide the Service.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — authentication and database infrastructure. Encrypted, secure.

All providers are contractually bound to process your data only on our behalf. We may disclose data if required by law.

  • Account data — retained for the duration of your account plus 30 days after deletion.
  • Scan results and leak records — retained while your account is active. Deleted on account deletion.
  • Payment records — retained 7 years as required by financial regulations (managed by Paddle).
  • Server logs — maximum 90 days, then automatically purged.
  • Anonymous scan sessions — usernames not stored. Purged immediately after scan completion.

7. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of all your personal data.
  • Portability — receive your data in machine-readable format.
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, where processing is consent-based.

Email privacy@vantage.co to exercise any right. We respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for authentication and session management. No tracking, advertising, or third-party analytics cookies.

9. Data Security

  • All data in transit encrypted via TLS 1.3.
  • Passwordless authentication — no passwords ever stored.
  • Database access restricted via row-level security (RLS) — you can only access your own data.
  • API endpoints are rate-limited and abuse-protected.

In the event of a data breach affecting you, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by law.

10. Children's Privacy

VANTAGE is exclusively for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Contact legal@vantage.co immediately if you believe we have.

We will notify registered users by email of any material changes. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
